Tai Cheong Bakery

Most well-known egg tart shop across the city. They have multiple stores: Sheung Wan, Aberdeen, Wan Chai, the Peak, etc. Each branch makes egg tarts throughout the day, so I like to wait till a new batch comes out to get one. Fresh egg tarts out of the oven... yum!

Address: 35 Lyndhurst Terrace, Central
Phone: 8300 8301

Oddie's Foodies

Tbh their food is more #instaworthy than it is yummy. But kudos to their creative spins on the classic HK street-snack, the egg waffle. Their sweet combo of Italian gelato with HK egg waffle is an exemplar of old meets west.

Address: 45 Gough Street, Central
Phone: 2570 2111

Via Tokyo

Must try their matcha soft serve. I love ice cream and I love matcha, so naturally they've won me over. When my best friends from Tufts visited HK, both said that out of all the desserts they tried during their trip, they liked the matcha soft serve from Via Tokyo the most!

Address: Shop 1A-1B, G/F, Leishun Court, 106-126 Leighton Road, Causeway Bay
Phone: 2895 1116

Cobo House by 2am: dessertbar

New artisan dessert bar in the city. Their innovative options are perfect for anyone with a sweet tooth like me!

Address: G/F, 8-12 South Lane, Shek Tong Tsui, Western District
Phone: 2656 3088

Sevva

With an outdoor terrace that has a great view of the financial district and Victoria Harbor, Sevva offers a great location for high tea or drinks.

Address: 25/F, Prince's Building, 10 Chater Road, Central, Hong Kong
Phone: 2537 1388

Lab Made Ice Cream

Made to order liquid nitrogen ice cream, so their ice cream is super fresh, smooth and creamy. The flavours never get boring because Lab Made invents new ones each week!

Address: G/F, 6 Brown Street, Tai Hang
Phone: 2670 0071
