Home Eat To Live

New vegetarian restaurant in Central. Sustainably-sourced, delicious plant-based food in a fast casual location. They sell veggie bowls, salad bowls and burgers as well as make-your-own oven-fired flatbreads, a panoply of vegan baked goods, ferments, cold-pressed juices and smoothies, vegan burgers, non-dairy ice cream (launching soon) and desserts.

Address: Ground Floor, Nexxus Building, 77 Des Voeux Road, Central
Phone: 2777 4777
